About Intellecta

A smarter way to practice mathematics

📚 What is Intellecta?

Intellecta is an interactive mathematics quiz platform designed for students studying core calculus and algebra topics. Whether you're preparing for exams or sharpening your skills, Intellecta offers timed, topic-specific challenges across multiple difficulty levels.

🎯 Topics Covered

📈 Limits & Continuity

Convergence, L'Hôpital's rule, and continuity theorems.

∂ Differentiation

Chain rule, implicit differentiation, higher-order derivatives.

∫ Integration

Definite & indefinite integrals, substitution, by-parts.

⬛ Matrices

Determinants, eigenvalues, rank, and matrix operations.

ℂ Complex Numbers

Argand plane, modulus-argument form, Euler's identity.

⚙️ How It Works

  1. Choose your difficulty level — Beginner, Intermediate, or Pro.
  2. Pick the topics you want to be tested on.
  3. Answer questions within a 30-second timer per question.
  4. Get instant colour-coded feedback on each answer.
  5. See your score, stats, and performance badge at the end.

🏆 Difficulty Levels

🟢 Beginner

Fundamental concepts and direct formula applications.

🟡 Intermediate

Multi-step problems requiring conceptual understanding.

🔴 Pro Level

Advanced theory, proofs, and complex problem-solving.
